# 📊 Wavecon Telecom Performance Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes the business performance of **Wavecon Telecom** before and after the **5G launch** using an interactive **Power BI Dashboard**.

The objective of this analysis is to evaluate how the 5G rollout impacted key business metrics such as Revenue, ARPU, Active Users, Churn, Plan Performance, and City-wise trends.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
After launching 5G services, Wavecon Telecom wanted to understand:

- Did revenue increase after 5G launch?
- Which KPIs improved or declined?
- Which plans are performing well after 5G?
- Which cities are underperforming?
- What strategic actions are required?

---

## 📊 Tools Used

- Power BI  
- Power Query  
- DAX  
- Data Cleaning  
- Data Visualization  

---

## 📈 Key KPIs Analyzed

- Total Revenue  
- ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)  
- TAU (Total Active Users)  
- TUSU (Total Unsubscribed Users)  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### 📌 Revenue Impact
- Revenue before 5G: **₹16bn**
- Revenue after 5G: **₹15.9bn**
- Slight decline of **0.5%**

### 📌 ARPU Performance
- Increased from **₹190.2** to **₹211.3**
- Indicates higher revenue per user

### 📌 Customer Base
- TAU declined from **84.4M** to **77.4M**
- TUSU increased from **5.6M** to **7M**

### 📌 High Performing Plans
- P1 – Smart Recharge Pack  
- P11 – Ultra Fast Mega Pack  
- P12 – Ultra Duo Data Pack  
- P13 – Mini Ultra Saver Pack  

### 📌 Geographical Trends
Some major cities like delhi,chennai showed weaker post-5G performance, highlighting market-specific challenges.

---

## 💡 Recommendations

- Improve customer retention strategies
- Redesign underperforming plans
- Increase 5G adoption campaigns
- Focus on underperforming cities
- Optimize pricing strategy
